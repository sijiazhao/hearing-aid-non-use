Welcome

Thank you for taking the time to complete this questionnaire. We are interested in learning about your experience with hearing aids.

Your answers will help us understand what works well and what could be improved, so that we can better support people who use hearing aids.

  • This should take about 5–10 minutes
  • Your responses are anonymous
  • There are no right or wrong answers — we want to hear about your personal experience

If you have any questions, please speak to the clinician who gave you this questionnaire.
