# Listening, energy, and daily life

Draft section for the hearing aid experience questionnaire. Removed from v1 (pilot) but kept for potential future use.

These questions map onto the HHIA → LEEAS → FSS → AMI pathway.

## Questions

### 1. Listening effort (maps to LEEAS)

**How much effort does it take to follow conversations in your daily life?**

- No effort at all (1)
- A little effort (2)
- Moderate effort (3)
- A lot of effort (4)
- Extreme effort — I have to concentrate very hard (5)

### 2. Effort–fatigue link (LEEAS → FSS)

**Do you feel that the effort of listening contributes to your overall tiredness?**

- Not at all (1)
- A little (2)
- Moderately (3)
- Quite a bit (4)
- Very much — listening wears me out (5)

### 3. General fatigue (maps to FSS)

**How tired or fatigued do you generally feel by the end of a typical day?**

- Not at all tired (1)
- A little tired (2)
- Moderately tired (3)
- Very tired (4)
- Exhausted (5)

### 4. Motivation change (maps to AMI behavioural)

**Have you noticed any changes in your motivation to do everyday activities (e.g., housework, hobbies, going out)?**

- No change — I'm as motivated as I've always been (1)
- Slightly less motivated than before (2)
- Noticeably less motivated (3)
- Much less motivated — I often don't get around to things (4)
- I rarely feel motivated to do anything (5)

### 5. Social avoidance (maps to AMI social / LEEAS → social path)

**Do you find yourself avoiding social situations because listening feels too effortful?**

- Never (1)
- Occasionally (2)
- Sometimes (3)
- Often (4)
- Very often — I avoid most social situations (5)

### 6. Hearing aids and effort (intervention effect)

**On days you wear your hearing aids, does listening feel less effortful compared to days you don't?**

- Much less effortful with hearing aids (1)
- Slightly less effortful (2)
- No difference (3)
- More effortful with hearing aids (4)
- Not applicable — I always / never wear them (5)
